### Step 1: start with the problem
- what problem we are trying to solve?
	- objective: what are we trying to achieve
	- audience: who exactly are we talking to; use claude for this
	- message: what do we want them to think, feel, or do?
- these three must me aligned before any channel or budget decisions are made

Step 1:  key questions to align on before anything else
- what is the primary goal, and what real-world impact are we trying to make
- who is the audience, demographics
- how do we coordinate efficiently and strategically
- budget, and is it realistic for the objective
- the time line, external moments(new cycles, policy windows) we need to plan around?
- what creative do we have, or what do we need to make?
- where are we sending people when they click, and is that landing page ready? if social, do we need verification?
- note: team work across paid, strategic comms, and creative teams. client gut checks are necessary
