## Small Talk
- critical in interpersonal interactions.
- the goal of small talk is create rapport. rapport is getting along well oith others by having **things in common**, thus making the communication flow easier
	- friends in common
	- same college
	- types of music
	- personal interests
	- sports
- another way to build rapport: **asking questions** but non-intrusive questions, and taking time to listen.
	- birth place
	- occupation
	- goas
	- hobbies
- **ARE(Anchor, Reveal, and Encourage)**
	- Anchor: observe the mutual shared with other person. How's the game?
	- reveal: open more information
	- encourage: asking questions: what's your favorite football team?
